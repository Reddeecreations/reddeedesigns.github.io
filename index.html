Behind The Press

From The Boro to The Bay

"Red Dee‑lightful is a hometown sublimation studio built by two Erie‑area girls — Red from the Bay and Dee from the Boro. Together, we create custom gifts that feel like they came straight from the heart of Northwestern Pennsylvania."

Our designs are inspired by everything that makes our region unforgettable: Lake Erie sunsets, Presque Isle summers, Edinboro charm, 814 pride, local landmarks, and the everyday life of our tight‑knit community. We also create seasonal collections for every holiday and work one‑on‑one with customers to bring their ideas to life.

Every mug, tumbler, tote, puzzle, and personalized gift is crafted with the same spirit that built this business — Your Imagination, Our Creations.
